**REMINDER: Always export/import configs when switching firmware to avoid losing settings.**

### T-Deck

#### Official

- MeshOS - On-device only (no BLE or USB)
  - On-device remote administration
  - Requires activation key to unlock advanced features such as remote administration
- Ripple - On-device only (no BLE or USB)
- Community firmware - BLE only

#### Alternative

- ⭐ [MCTerm](https://github.com/dabeani/meshcore) - On-device + BLE/WiFi/USB
    - Select repeater from Contacts, swipe left or right for remote administration
    - `-merged` for initial installation, non-merged for updates
- [Launcher](https://bmorcelli.github.io/Launcher/webflasher.html) - Switch between multiple firmwares
  - Supports MeshOS, Ripple, and MCTerm + Meshtastic firmwares

### Wio Tracker L1 Pro

#### Official
- Ripple - On-device only (no BLE or USB)
- Community firmware - BLE only or USB only

#### Alternative

- ⭐ [WhisperOS](https://ssaprus.works/flasher) - On-device + BLE
  - Issues with emojis, rendered as Chinese characters
  - Can connect with official MeshCore Android app, but not with alternate apps
